What this is

This is a free, educational tool. It reads market price data and shows you support and resistance levels, a trend view across timeframes, and a momentum reading. It is meant to help you see the market more clearly. It is not a trading platform, and it does not place trades.

Nothing here is advice, a recommendation, or a signal to buy or sell anything. The tool never tells you what will happen next. Every decision you make with it is your own. If you want advice, talk to a licensed financial professional who knows your situation.

Trading is risky

Trading forex and similar markets carries a high risk of losing money, and many people do. Levels and trends that held in the past can break at any time. Past patterns do not predict future prices. Only risk money you can afford to lose.

The data can be wrong or delayed

Prices and the economic calendar come from third-party data providers and may be inaccurate, incomplete, or delayed. The news markers under the chart show real scheduled events, but they only describe the calendar — they never predict what price will do. Do not rely on anything here for a live trading decision without checking it against your own broker and sources.
